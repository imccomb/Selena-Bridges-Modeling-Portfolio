Selena Bridges

Model. Actress. Artist.

Model

Selena is an accomplished model who has done photoshoots with the likes of high-profile publications such as Vogue Magazine, Essence and more.

Actress

Selena has taken professional acting classes and has starred in local Broadway productions. Her acting in these productions were met with critical acclaim from both critics and the audience.

Artist

Selena is a gifted vocalist; her voice was lauded by critics in the Broadway musical she starred in last year. Her artistry goes beyond singing; she is also a gifted pianist and songwriter.

About

A Missouri native, Selena was born on May 21, 1993. From a very early age, Selena loved to be the center of attention; she liked for everyone to be focused on her, to be in the spotlight, the star.

Selena began modeling at the age of 14; she did well in the career despite being shorter than the average model. Selena has continued to model while building a resume in Broadway; she has starred in Broadway adaptations of hits such as The Color Purple and The Wiz: Live.

Selena Bridges describes herself as a feminist who is not afraid to discuss topics such as beauty, the sexualization of women and girls, body image and positivity, art and politics.
